The King's Arms: At the Heart of Polebrook

For over three centuries, the King's Arms has stood at the centre of Polebrook life. It's where generations have gathered to celebrate, commiserate, and simply be together. More than bricks and mortar, it represents the soul of our community.

Now, this irreplaceable heritage landmark faces an uncertain future. Without action, we risk losing not just a building, but a living connection to our shared history and the gathering place that binds our village together.

We cannot let that happen.

What's Happening

The King's Arms faces a critical moment. Economic pressures and changing circumstances have put its future in jeopardy. The clock is ticking, and without significant community support, closure becomes increasingly likely.

A Heritage Worth Protecting

Three hundred years of history reside within these walls. From the coaching inn days to the present, the King's Arms has witnessed every chapter of Polebrook's story. It's a listed building, a local landmark, and an irreplaceable piece of English heritage.
